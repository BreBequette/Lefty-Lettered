Welcome

I'm Bre. I like to make things.

I make all sorts of things: baked goods, whatever DIY project du jour has my interest, I've even been known to make the occasional scene. But I really like to make one-of-a-kind hand-lettered decor for homes and weddings. Keep scrolling to get to know more about me and what I do.

Weddings

Fun fact: I got into lettering so I could DIY more of my own wedding. I did my own calligraphy and decor, and now I love helping other St. Louisans make their wedding dreams a reality.

Chalkboards

These days, what's an event without a welcome chalkboard? I've done boards for a variety of different occasions and they're always a fun way to welcome guests to your party.

Custom Projects

Some of my favorite projects are the ones that don't fit neatly into a category – it's about bringing a client's specific idea to life, just for them.
